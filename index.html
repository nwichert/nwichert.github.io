Our Approach

We are a venture studio that invests in great entreprenurs to scale viable startups.

Our network consists of entrepreneurs, corporations, private equity, corporate venture capital, and angle investors to source ideas, rapidly validate concepts in our proven rigorous process, and co-invest in the most promissing business models.

Entrepreneurs Our in-house team of venture builders will stand up new ventures with proven entrepreneurs to help scale the most promising ventures in our portfolio.

Co-Creation We work with our network to identify opportunities and short circuit learning curves to build and invest in high-growth opportunities that solve real problems.

Investment Capital Our venture studio is funded by some of the best entrepreneurs and corporations in the Midwest to create a robust portfolio of investable ventures.

Brand Firewall Our structure allows corporations to co-create with us in a way that pushes the bounds of innovation without putting your current brand, customers, or sales channel at risk.

What we do

Full-Service Venture Studio

We are startup factory, highly calibrated and incentivized to bring only the best ventures to market quickly. We design and build amazing products that address emerging markets and solve tough problems. We find strong customer pull through rigorous validation and digital marketing. To be as efficient as possible, we also manage the books, legal, and HR of each venture through our shared services model.

Jobs-to-be-Done

We're sticklers on finding and understanding job executors to validate a job.

Concept Validation

We rapidly prototype high- and low-fidelity prototypes to test key aspects of the business model.

Engineering

We deliver a marketable SaaS products that generate revenue.

Go-to-Market

Our focus is to find viable unit economics to raise follow on growth capital.

Fundraising

Our fund invests and helps raise additional follow on funding for each successful venture we spin out.

Finance & Operations

We make sure entrepreneurs focus on running the business not managing Quickbooks.

Legal & HR

From inception to first round of funding, we provide all the legal and HR support to scale each venture.

...and More!

From Product Managerment, to Visual and UX Design, we source all the required talent to ensure we give each venture a shot a growth.

Get Beyond Innovation Theater

Building a great startup is hard. It requires experience, uncommon talent, great execution, and the ever-elusive great idea. Our team has the pedigree and track record to rapidly validate, refine, and build new ventures.
