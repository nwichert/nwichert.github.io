Venture Studio Operating Model

Venture Studios are an evolution of innovation programming over the past decade. They're not incubators, not accelerators, not venture capitalists, and definitely are not your typical company. They entrepreneurial run and very efficient at creating new ventures to help companies innovate.

Holding Entity Model.

New startups are financed through our studio itself as a Holding Entity (e.g., C-Corp). The equity owned in each venture is on the balance sheet of the Holding Entity.

Drawing the curtain on innovation theater

To create capacity and room to innovate, the venture studio will work with companies to establish separate entities for each venture it creates. We work with companies to fund innovation off the balance sheet while maintaining equity in the venture in order to particiate in the future growth of the venture.

Unlike work for hire consultancies, our goal is to help you start a company. Our align incentive structure means we'll be agile and look for opportunities we mutually see as impactful. If an idea starts to loose momemtum or is proven invalid during early testing, we'll shut it down and recoup leftover capital to start pursuing a new venture.

Our holding entity model structure helps corporations align the incentives of entrepreneurs and the growth objectives of the company. The goal of the studio in partnership with the company, is to create a porfolio of ventures that can leverage the most appropriate type of scale: outsourced via the studio, integration into company, investment into an opperating team, or other creative solutions.
